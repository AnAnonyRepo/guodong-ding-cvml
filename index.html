Guodong Ding丁国栋
Senior Research Fellow
School of Computing (SoC) · National University of Singapore

My research focuses on understanding human actions in long-range, multi-step video sequences, particularly temporal action segmentation. Recently I have been interested in applying these techniques to medical video understanding (coronary angiography, surgical workflow) and robot learning. I closely collaborate with Prof. Angela Yao at NUS CVML.
